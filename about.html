Who I Am

I am currently the fourth year at University of California, San Diego. I am majoring in Cognitive Science specialized in Human-Computer Interaction. My interest in brain and human behavior introduced me to study cognitive science at UCSD. I believe the user experience is the study of complex human behavior in response to a relationship with technology. As user experience designer, I love applying ideas from my head into the real world, and I have a strong passion in design that is not only visually appealing but also user-friendly because the ultimate goal of the design is at people.


My goal, not only as a designer, but also as an individual, is to learn and get inspired from different fields, cultures, and people. I am looking forward to more opportunities to gain valuable experiences.


I am originally from South Korea and currently live in sunny San Diego. When I am not working on my designs, I like to spend my time on reading novels, traveling the world, and watching movies.
